Summary Takeaways

- Consistent profit growth from 2021 to 2023.
- Copiers and Phones are the top-performing products.
- Tables and Bookcases are major loss drivers in the Furniture category.
- California outperforms all other states, while Texas has the largest losses.
- Consumer segment drives both highest revenue and profit, with a lower return rate than Corporate or Home Office.
- Return rate improvement across all segments is a positive operational signal.
